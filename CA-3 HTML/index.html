Soya Chaap

Ingredients

  • Soya Chaap
  • Cooking Oil
  • Tomato Paste
  • Spices
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Gram flour (besan)
  • Garam masala

Recipe

  1. Marinate soya chaap in curd, ginger-garlic paste, gram flour, Kashmiri red chili powder, and salt.
  2. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours.
  3. Skewer the marinated chaap and grill or cook in a pan until browned.
  4. In a separate pan, heat oil, add garam masala and kasuri methi
  5. Add the cooked soya chaap and toss until coated
  6. Serve hot as a snack or with naan/roti.

Recipe

  4. Rinse flattened rice once or twice and then add to the rice.
  5. Pour 2 cups water. Stir and soak both rice and flattened rice together for 4 to 5 hours
  6. Before grinding, strain the water from the soaked lentils+fenugreek seeds and keep it for grinding urad dal. You can also use fresh water if you prefer
  7. Remove batter in a bowl or pan with a spatula. Below is a photo showing the consistency of the urad dal batter.
  8. Strain the rice well. Then in the same grinder, add the soaked rice and 1 cup fresh water. You can grind rice in one batch or in two to three batches. This will depend on the size of the grinder jar.
  9. Grind the rice till you get a fine grainy consistency in it. The rice batter should have a fine rava (cream of wheat) like consistency of the ground rice grains
  10. Break up boiled potato into small chunks and add them to your mixture. Add water and stir.
