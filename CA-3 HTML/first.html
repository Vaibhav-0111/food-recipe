Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya (reduced milk)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, flour, and baking soda to make a smooth dough.
  2. Shape into small balls and fry in ghee until golden brown.
  3. Boil sugar and water to make a syrup, add cardamom.
  4. Soak fried balls in syrup for 2 hours.

Jalebi

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yogurt
  • Ghee
  • Saffron strands
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Mix flour, yogurt, ghee, and saffron to form a batter.
  2. Let it ferment for 12 hours
  3. Prepare sugar syrup with water and cardamom.
  4. Pour batter in concentric circles in hot oil, fry until crisp
  5. Soak in sugar syrup for a few minutes

Sandesh

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Saffron strands

Recipe

  1. Knead paneer, sugar, cardamom into a dough
  2. Shape into discs, garnish with saffron.
  3. Chill and serve.

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Lemon juice or vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Boil milk, add lemon juice to curdle.
  2. Strain and knead the chenna (paneer) into soft dough
  3. Shape into small balls.
  4. Boil sugar and water, add cardamom.
  5. Cook chenna balls in the syrup until they double in size.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Khoya (reduced milk)
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom powder
  • Almonds or pistachios

Recipe

  1. Cook khoya and sugar in ghee until it thickens
  2. Add cardamom and mix well
  3. Spread in a greased tray, garnish with nuts.
  4. Let it cool and set, cut into squares.

Kheer

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Almonds and raisins

Recipe

  1. Cook rice in milk until soft and mushy.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom, simmer until thick.
  3. Garnish with almonds and raisins.
  4. Serve chilled or warm.
